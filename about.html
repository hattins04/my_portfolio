SOME THINGS ABOUT ME

My name is Sam Hattin, I am from the Greater Toronto Area but I have lived a handful of places in the past 7 years, both in Canada and the United States. I do web design, focusing specifically on front-end UI (User Interface) web development. As a freelance front-end web developer, I create from scratch clean, accessible, responsive and cross-browser compatible web applications that also looks good on Tablets, iPads and Smartphones.

I fell inlove with web development in 2015 and I have not stopped coding since. I continue to learn everyday and impliment new strategies and practices in my work. One day I hope to be working with a team of like minded developers on something ridiculously cool!
